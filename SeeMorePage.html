WHAT IS SURPRISING ABOUT IT?
My culture shock in computer science stemmed from the stark shift in problem-solving approaches. Coming from a non-technical background, I was initially overwhelmed by the precision and logic required in coding. The collaborative and open-source nature of the field was also a pleasant surprise, as it was different from the more competitive environment I was used to. It took time to adapt to these cultural nuances.
